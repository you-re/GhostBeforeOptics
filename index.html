About

Ghost Before Optics is a 3D design and animation studio based in Helsinki, Finland. We specialize in 3D animation, simulation, and post-production, creating photorealistic multimedia content for commercials, advertisements, social media, films, and immersive experiences like mixed and virtual reality.

Whether you’re an emerging brand looking to build recognition or an established name aiming to push creative boundaries, we help bring your vision to life with captivating, high-quality visuals that make an impact. Let’s create something extraordinary together.
